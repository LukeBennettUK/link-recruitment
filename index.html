About Us

Find out why you should choose Link-recruitment

Link-recruitment are an East Midlands based recruitment agency that specialise in Customer Service, Sales, IT and Technology however are not limited to this.

We truly understand the importance of building relationships with clients as well as candidates, allowing us to fully understand hiring needs and never compromising on quality. We reward and recognise success.

We exhibit a moral and ethnic responsibility to both our clients and candidates; by meeting these needs, we create meaningful opportunities for employees and long-term value for business.

We are friendly, professional recruiters who handle a wide range of vacancies whether that may be temporary, temporary to permanent or permanent positions.

What makes us different?

We’re small enough to care but big enough to make an impact. Genuine enthusiasm and passion for what we do, coupled with our ability to respond and adapt quickly to our clients needs.

Employers choose to work with Link-recruitment because we realise that no two vacancies are the same and every job vacancy matters to us. We pride ourselves on our honest, ethical and professional approach to recruitment.

Our thorough screening process ensures that you will receive a highly targeted shortlist of relevant and local applicants who will have been interviewed and fully briefed about the vacancy by one of our experienced recruiters.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to our people- through recognition and also rewards. Our values underpin everything that we do and this is reflected in our people.
